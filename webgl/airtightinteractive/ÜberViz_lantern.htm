ÜBERVIZ LIVE DEMO

Überviz is a generative realtime Music Visualizer. Multiple layered audio-reactive visuals automatically sync to incoming audio from the microphone or MP3. Visuals and Post-Processing FX are selected to create a non-repeating progression over time. Audio Reactivity is achieved by processing a combination of audio inputs: Volume, Frequency Levels, Audio Waveform, Beat Detection and BPM.

Automatic Mode

By default Überviz automatically works with the incoming audio. Viz and FXs switch out based on beat detection.

Manual Mode

Hit 'Q' to toggle Control Panel. Uncheck 'AUTOMATIC' for manual control. Use 'AUDIO' 'Gain' to adjust for source volume. Tap 'W' to set BPM. Use 'VIZ' sliders to adjust audio-reactive Viz parameters. Use 'FX' sliders to adjust post-processing effects.
